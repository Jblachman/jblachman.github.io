Whistler Blackhomb Mountain Skiing Guide

Whistler Blackhomb is a family friendly mountain located about 2 hours Northeast of Vancouver, CA. It is around 5 hours north of Seattle, WA. While expensive, Whistler Blackhomb is the gold-standard of both mountain conditions and resort quality. There is a reason Whistler Blackhomb is known as one of the top ski resorts in the world. With a cute, busy town in the mountain village, there are always activities both during the day and the night. Whistler is a bucket-list location for the avid skiier.
