Summit Snoqualmie Mountain Skiing Guide

Summit Snoqualmie is a family friendly mountain located about 40 minutes east of Seattle. Most of the mountain is kid-friendly resort, but there are difficult runs scattered around the mountain. There is Summit East, Summit West, and Alpental. Alpental has more variety of runs for advanced skiiers. The family-friendly mountain set-up makes Summit Snoqualmie the ideal day ski-trip.
